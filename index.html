ABOUT ME


I am Shubham Vashisth, a final year engineering student majoring in Computer Science from Amity University, Noida, U.P., India (85.3% till 7th semester). Being a Computer Science student, I feel it's all about using the fundamental, theoretical, and practical concepts in solving real-world problems. I love to expand my knowledge space with the diversity of subjects within the field of computer science and its intersection with other scientific areas. I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My work is mostly focused on Supervised Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Quantum Computing.

My Motivation & journey in AI

The choice to pursue a career in the research area of artificial intelligence after completing my undergraduate study was because of my passion to bring the maximum out of the undiscovered applications within this field that can be used for the betterment of mankind. I am always eager to analyze various mathematical and statistical models which along with data produce intelligent systems that are used for solving classification, regression, pattern recognition, and many more problems.

I have devoted more than 3 years to the domain of AI and machine learning and have expanded my conceptual knowledge by doing various certifications in this domain followed by publishing quality research papers during my undergraduate studies.

Fostering research in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning


I have always promoted the research in AI and its subfields in the computer science community and among my fellow juniors. This includes me delivering guest lectures, leading various committees, participating in several International AI conferences and much more.
